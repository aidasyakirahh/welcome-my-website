Cameron Highland,Pahang

The cafe’s most famous dish is ‘Nasi Lemak Sambal Strawberry’, a Malaysian favourite of coconut-milk infused rice, salted anchovies and crispy fried chicken with an added twist to its sambal (chilli condiment) that uses strawberries.
